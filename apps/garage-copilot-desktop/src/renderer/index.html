    About DeepScan

    A read-only diagnostic tool for your car. It plugs into the OBD-II port through a standard ELM327 adapter (USB or Bluetooth) using the Web Serial API, and turns what the car reports into a clear diagnostic picture. It never clears codes, writes to the ECU, or runs active tests.

    • Diagnose — MIL status, trouble codes (with structural decode), I/M readiness, live snapshot.
    • Live Monitor — stream key parameters and flag fuel-trim drift, overheating, and weak charging.
    • Tune Advisor — validate a proposed change (gearing, injectors, electrical load) before committing.

    Everything shown is evidence to verify against service information — not a definitive repair diagnosis. The "tune" tools are planning math only; flashing an ECU is done with a proper licensed tool, and on-road emissions tuning is regulated.
